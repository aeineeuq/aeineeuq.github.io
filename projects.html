Post-2019 / BMA Engineering Projects

The Diversity Enabling Program of projects aims to support BHP's aspirational goal to achieve gender balance by 2025. As the Program Manager, I developed and executed activities relating to end to end project management, including: creating project schedules and cost estimates, presenting business cases to gain capital funding and supporting teams to execute projects across multiple mine sites.

Autonomous Haulage is expected to create a safer work environment at BMA sites. As a Project Engineer, I assisted in the safe and timely execution of civil construction works onsite to enable the improvement of the technology infrastructure. This including installing communication towers, installation of 12km fibre optic cabling and building autonomous truck calibration pads.

TLO Automation seeks to move from a manual, operator-controlled Train Load-Out (TLO) system to an unmanned, fully automated train loading capability. As a Project Engineer, I assisted in facilitating the on-site project execution to replace and update the train load out weighbridge at Goonyella Riverside Mine.
